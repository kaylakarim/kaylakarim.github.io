all About me

who am i?

I am currently a fourth-year student at Fordham University in New York City, pursuing a Bachelor’s degree in New Media and Digital Design. Throughout my academic journey and professional experiences, I've acquired a diverse skill set encompassing design principles, typography, motion design, web development, UX/UI design, and 3D design. These skills have molded me into a versatile designer capable of tackling various creative challenges.

My design philosophy revolves around a balance between visual appeal and readability, always keeping the audience's needs and intentions at the forefront. Whether I'm crafting an event advertisement or a personal motion graphic project, I prioritize user experience and intentionality in my designs. I lean towards maximalism, enjoying the process of layering effects, shapes, and text to create visually rich compositions. However, my experiences working with prestigious companies have taught me the art of minimalism, enabling me to deliver clean and concise designs when necessary.

I am perpetually seeking new opportunities to push the boundaries of my craft and expand my skill set.
