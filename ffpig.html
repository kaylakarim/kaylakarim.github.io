Instagram Graphics

During my time at Fashion for Philanthropy, I've developed a variety of graphics for upcoming events, recaps of past events, and other essential updates.

Working with Fashion for Philanthropy has given me the freedom to experiment with diverse design styles and unleash my creativity. In my role as their social media coordinator, I essentially functioned as the creative director, responsible for crafting all Instagram graphics, flyers, pamphlets, and more.

Given that a significant portion of our outreach to students occurs through social media platforms, crafting posts that effectively share information about upcoming and past events is critical.
