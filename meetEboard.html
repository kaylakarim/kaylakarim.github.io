SEPTEMBER 2023

Meet the E-board

A set of motion graphics I created unveiling Fashion for Philanthropy's Fall 2023 E-board. Drawing inspiration from the iconic aesthetics of old Apple UI, I meticulously customized every detail (backgrounds and windows) using Photoshop.

This project was the first time I played around with a 3D typeface, brought to life through dynamic animations and artful glitches meticulously crafted in After Effects. This project also allowed me to test a more digital, y2k design style. Through this visual journey, I not only showcase the E-board but display each of the members distinct personalities.
