DECEMBER 2022

Midnights Top Tracks

A personal project that I created after the release of Midnights in 2022. The initial idea was sparked by the album's unique color palettes and typography, as well as the unveiling of my 2022 Spotify Wrapped. This inspiration fueled the creation of this animation showcasing, my top tracks from the album, crafted using After Effects. This project served as both a creative outlet and tribute to the album.
