Holiday Placecards

At the annual office holiday party hosted by Nike Communications, I took on the responsibility of designing place cards for every dish, drink, and snack featured at the event. Mindful of our luxurious brand identity, I curated each card with attention to detail, opting for a sophisticated, intricate border design.

In line with the festive spirit, I maintained a harmonious color palette, drawing from classic holiday hues to ensure visual cohesion throughout.
